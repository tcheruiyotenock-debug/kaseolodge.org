At Football Live TV, accessible from kaseolodge.org, one of our main priorities is the privacy of our visitors. This Privacy Policy document contains types of information that is collected and recorded by Football Live TV and how we use it.

1. Information We Collect

Football Live TV is designed to provide sports updates with minimal data collection. We may collect:

  • Device Information: Technical data such as your device model and operating system version.
  • Favorites: Selected teams and leagues stored locally to personalize your experience.
  • Device Identifiers: We may use the Android Advertising ID to provide personalized advertisements and for internal analytics.

2. Background Data Usage

Our app uses background services (via Workmanager) to provide real-time goal alerts and news updates. This process checks for score updates at regular intervals. No personal browsing data or location data is tracked during this process.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information to provide, operate, and maintain the App, improve user experience, send push notifications for match events, and display relevant advertisements through partners like Google AdMob.

4. Third-Party Services

We use third-party services that may collect information used to identify you, including: Google AdMob, OneSignal, Firebase, and specialized Sports Data APIs.

5. Children's Privacy

Football Live TV does not knowingly collect any Personal Identifiable Information from children under the age of 13. If you believe a child has provided information, please contact us immediately.
